The Best Movies According to Blossom

My top 5 movies of all time.

Person of Interest

This is my favorite American series. It's about saving people.

Criminal Minds

Really insightful series. It's about different serial killers and profilers.

Snowdrop

A very beautiful Korean series. Teaches about sacrifice.

Zootopia

One of my favorite cartoons of all time. Really beautiful and teaches about not giving up.

Encanto

Super beautiful cartoon. Teaches about love.
